About Us
Since 2006, together with a group of drivers, we have been providing licensed passenger transport services. These mainly include airport transfers (picking up guests from airports across Poland with a welcome sign), business transport (handling conferences and meetings), as well as private transport (e.g. for holidays, weddings, or other events). Companies from Japan, China, Scandinavia, Germany, Italy, as well as domestic enterprises have trusted us.
